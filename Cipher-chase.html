CIPHER CHASE

"Jump onto different computers,crack the codes and reveal the secret"

Description :


Cipher Chase is an engaging competition where teams of two put their code-cracking skills to the test. Participants embark on a journey to decode a series of challenges across three systems.


Rules :


Team Setup:
  • Teams can participate in solo or duo for this event.
    • How the Event Works:
    1. Upon entering the venue, teams receive a code to decipher manually, resulting in a 10-characters output.
    2. The first two digits of the number represent the system number, while the remaining eight digits form the password to access that system.
    3. Teams face a cycle of challenges within each accessed system, with another code challenge presented with more hindraces.
    4. This cycle continues until teams successfully navigate through a series of systems.
    Winning Criteria:
  • The team that successfully cracks codes across all systems and completes the challenges with the highest proficiency wins the event.
