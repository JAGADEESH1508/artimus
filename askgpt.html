Description :


In "Ask GPT," teams of two or individuals aim to make ChatGPT say a specific word without directly using that word in their conversation. They must guide ChatGPT towards generating the target word through clever questioning and context, avoiding direct mentions of the word itself.


Rules :


Team Setup:
  • Participants can compete solo or in teams of two.
    • How the Event Works:
    1. Each participant or team is provided with a designated word.
    2. Participants engage in conversation with ChatGPT, aiming to prompt it to generate the assigned word without directly stating it.
    3. Communication with ChatGPT must avoid direct mentions of the assigned word.
    4. Time limits are enforced for each interaction with ChatGPT to ensure prompt gameplay.
    5. Each round contains a special challenge which will be announced on the spot.
    Winning Criteria:
  • The team or Individuals who win the 2nd round will be crowned the winner.
