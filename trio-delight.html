Trio Delight

"3 different games with increasing difficulties crafted to make the participants delighted"

Description :

"Join us for Trio Delight, where solo players compete in three rounds of exhilarating challenges. With time limits adding urgency, participants showcase their skills, teamwork, and quick thinking. Only one will emerge victorious in the electrifying grand finale. Don't miss out on the fun, competition, and glory!"

Team Setup:

  • This is a solo event. Participants must compete individually.

How the Event Works:

  1. Round 1 - Warm-Up Fun:

    • Participants gather and get acquainted with the event format.
    • Engage in light-hearted icebreaker activities to kickstart the fun.
    • Quick, energetic games to get everyone warmed up and ready for the challenges ahead.
    • Time limit keeps the pace lively and exciting.

  2. Round 2 - Test of Skills:

    • Teams or individuals face off in a series of skill-based challenges.
    • Each challenge presents a unique test of coordination, strategy, and creativity.
    • Participants must think on their feet and work together to overcome obstacles.
    • Time limits add an element of urgency, keeping competitors on their toes.

  3. Round 3 - Grand Finale Showdown:

    • The top performers from previous rounds advance to the ultimate showdown.
    • Intense head-to-head competition as finalists battle it out for the title.
    • High-stakes challenges push participants to give it their all.
    • Time limits heighten the tension as the clock ticks down to determine the ultimate winner.

Winning:

  • Participant securing more points in the final round will be crowned the winners.
