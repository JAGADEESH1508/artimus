IPL Auction

"Flaunt your financial management skills combined with cricket to build an impeccable team"

Description :


Welcome to the ultimate showdown of cricket prowess! In this electrifying event, ten teams fiercely compete to assemble the most formidable lineup, each vying to bring together the perfect combination of batting, bowling, wicket-keeping, and all-round skills. With strategic bids and nail-biting negotiations, teams strive to craft the best roster, showcasing their tactical brilliance. Join us as the cricketing world holds its breath, anticipating the birth of the ultimate cricketing powerhouse in this thrilling battle of the IPL auction.

Rules :

  1. Team Members :2-3
  2. Any number of teams are allowed per college.
  3. Rounds : 1 st Round - IPL Quiz | 2 nd Round - Auction ( top 10 teams from IPL quiz ) .
  4. No phones are allowed during the IPL quiz .
  5. Bring your own pens, notes and refreshments , team paper will be provided .
  6. Winners will be selected based on overall rating of the team.
  7. Purse - 80 Crores, Squad - 13 to 16 players.

Event flow :

  1. After beginning the event, teams will attend a IPL Quiz and the top 10 teams will be selected for the IPL auction.
  2. Players will be bidded according to teams preference and ratings.
  3. A team should buy a minimum of 13 players and a maximum of 16 players which must consist of at-least 4 batters, 1 keeper, 3 allrounders , 3 bowlers.
  4. The best team is selected as winners based on Overall rating of the team.
  5. If two teams have the same rating, then the team with a high remaining purse will be the winner.
Winning Criteria:
  • The best rated team wins the First prize .
  • The Second best rated team will win the second prize.
