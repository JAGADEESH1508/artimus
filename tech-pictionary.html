Tech Pictionary

"Put together your drawing skills and technical skills to win exciting prizes"

Description :


Join us for Tech Pictionary, where teams of two compete in a fun-filled challenge of technical creativity! Put your drawing skills to the test as you sketch out technical terms for your teammate to guess. Get ready for a whirlwind of innovation and laughter as you race against the clock to decipher the drawings and emerge victorious!


Rules :


Participation Type :
  • Duo (Teams of two members only)
    • How the Event Works:


    Round 1 :
    1. Totally 30 questions.
    2. Pictures of logos are shown, and the team should guess the companies' logos.
    3. Teams will be selected for the next round based on their scores.
    Round 2 :
    1. teams will participate for a duration of 2 minutes.
    2. One team member will draw technical terms while the other member guesses them.
    3. Teams will be selected for the final round based on their scores.

    Round 3 :
    1. Teams will receive sentences related to the technical word that does not contain the actual word that is to be found.
    2. They must discuss among themselves and guess the word.
    3. The team with the highest score will be declared the winner.
    4. Teams will have time constraints.
    Winning Criteria:
  • The team with the highest cumulative score at the end of all rounds wins the competition.
