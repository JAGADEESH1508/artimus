Box Cricket

"Prove your cricket skills by playing it in a different environment with completely different rules."

Description :


Indoor Box Cricket is a unique twist on the classic game of cricket, designed for indoor settings and smaller groups of 6 players. It combines elements of traditional cricket with innovative rules tailored for indoor spaces, ensuring fast-paced action and maximum fun for participants of all ages.

Team Setup: 6 members per team

Overs: 4(four)

Rules :

  1. Underarm bowling
  2. Atleast 3 bowlers should be used
  3. In box cricket, the ball should not cross the boundary without pitching; it should not fly beyond the boundary entirely.
  4. Umpire decision is final
