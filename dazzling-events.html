Dazzling Delivery

"Showcase your new research work and get insights from intellects"

Description :


Dazzling Delivery invites participants to showcase their research, ideas, and innovations through captivating paper presentations. Dive into the world of academia and industry as you dazzle the audience with your insights and findings in this engaging contest!


Rules :


Participation Type :
  • Participants can compete solo or in teams of two.
    • Domains:
  • Internet of Things
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Deep Learning
  • Machine Learning
  • Data Science and Data Analytics
  • Cyber Security
  • Cloud Computing
  • Image Processing
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Embedded Systems
  • Speech Processing
  • Healthcare & Medicine
  • Bio-technology & Bio-Informants

    • How the Event Works:
    1. Participants present their papers on the preferred topic under specified domains.
    2. Each presentation follows your preferred format, covering research, innovations, or ideas.
    3. Presentations are delivered within a predetermined time limit.
    4. A question and answer session follows each presentation, allowing participants to address inquiries from judges.
    5. Maximum 10 slides.
    Winning Criteria:
  • Presentations are evaluated based on content quality, clarity, originality, and presentation skills.
  • The relevance of the topic to the domain will also influence scoring.
  • Winners are determined by the highest overall score awarded by the panel of judges.
