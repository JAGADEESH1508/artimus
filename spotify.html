"Show off your music knowledge in a competitive musical battle"

Description :


Join us for an exhilarating event where teams of 2-4 will test their music knowledge in a thrilling game of word association. Stay sharp as songs play and pause randomly, challenging teams to guess the word that follows. With escalating difficulty across three rounds, teams will race against the clock to claim victory. Are you ready to tune in and test your skills?"


Team Setup:

  • Teams consist of 2-4 members.

Event Flow:

  1. Teams gather as songs play and pause randomly.
  2. When the music pauses, teams must quickly identify the word that follows where the song stopped.
  3. Teams earn points for correctly guessing the word.
  4. Higher-scoring teams progress to subsequent rounds.
  5. Round 2 challenges teams to find 2 missing words, and Round 3 escalates to 3 missing words.
  6. Teams have a time limit to guess each word in Rounds 2 and 3.

Winning Criteria:

  • The team with the highest total points at the end of Round 3 wins the event.
